About Us

Eat Your Feelings is a cake shop based out of Ottawa. I started out of my home three years ago and it grew from catering to friends and family to craft sales and community events.

A flavour for every mood

All my flavors and names are based on moods, So if you are having a divorce party you could get the salty cake pop- that is devil food cake with salted caramel icing OR if you are having a baby shower you could get the excited cake pop that is confetti cake with fudge icing.
